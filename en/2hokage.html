Tobirama Senju

Who was he?

Tobirama Senju (千手扉間, Senju Tobirama) was the Second Hokage (二代目火影, Nidaime Hokage; Literally meaning "Second Fire Shadow") from Konohagakure. He comes from the Senju clan. Before inheriting the position of Hokage, he served as his brother's adviser helping him with political and social measures.

Physical Prowess and Chakra

Tobirama was hailed as the fastest shinobi during his lifetime, and indeed earned praise. Konoha's Yellow Lightning. While this recognition was due in large part to the his Space-Time Ninjutsu, Tobirama was able to move fast enough on account of to cover a target with explosive tags without him noticing.

Appearance of Tobirama Senju

Tobirama was a tall, fair-skinned man with shaggy white hair and blue eyes. dark color — depicted as red in the anime — and three red marks on the face — two under the eyes. eyes and one on the chin.

Tobirama Senju's Personality

Ever since he was a child, Tobirama was a stoic and composed individual with a logical and pragmatics for life. He was very obedient to his father, as well as loyal to his clan, but not enough to agree with all his decisions, as he was able to imagine a rational way for the shinobi clans to achieve peace.

Friend and rival

Izuna Uchiha.

Tobirama Senju's Abilities

Despite Hashirama being recognized as the "God of Shinobi", Tobirama was still one of most powerful shinobi of their era, earning praise from Madara Uchiha on multiple occasions; he was able to defeat Izuna Uchiha, a shinobi of equal skill and power to Madara. Danzō Shimura stated that Tobirama's prowess was unrivaled during his time as a Hokage. He even survived a battle against the Gold and Silver Brothers, who were imbued with the Nine-Tails' chakra and wielding the Valuable Tools of the Sage of Six Ways.

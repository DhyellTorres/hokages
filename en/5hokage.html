Who was she?

Tsunade (綱手, Tsunade) is one of the Legendary Sannin of Konohagakure. Although it is considered the world's most powerful kunoichi and greatest medical ninja, Tsunade has abandoned the life of a shinobi for many years in pursuit of other goals. She ends up being convinced to return to Konoha and becomes the Fifth Hokage.

As a legendary Iryō-nin, Tsunade is truly a role model for other Iryō-nin. follow. Your first priority in any situation is the lives of others.

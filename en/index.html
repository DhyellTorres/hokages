Hokage

What is a Hokage?

The Hokage (火影; literally meaning "Shadow of Fire") is the leader of Konohagakure. They are generally recognized as the strongest in the village, although ideology and reputation play a big role in being chosen for the position.

Who can become a Hokage?

Renowned ninjas who possess the will of fire and are recognized by the entire village.

How does one become a Hokage?

Usually, the new Hokage is selected by the current Hokage at the time of their retirement, but if the former Hokage dies or becomes incapacitated before being able to do so, the Fire Daimyō will appoint a Hokage candidate based on the recommendation of the Konoha Council and the Fire Daimyō's advisors. Afterward, the candidate must be approved by the village's jōnin to officially become Hokage.

What is the role of a Hokage?

The Hokage is the main representative of the Hidden Leaf Village (Konohagakure). The Hokage is responsible for ensuring the safety of all the village's residents.

Who was once a Hokage?

Six shinobi and one kunoichi won this title so far. Being them Hashirama Senju, Tobirama Senju, Hiruzen Sarutobi Minato Namikaze, Kakashi Hatake, Naruto Uzumaki and her Tsunade Senju.

Who are the main candidates to be the next Hokage?

Shikamaru, Konohamaru, Sarada and Boruto.

Minato Namikaze

Who was he?

Minato Namikaze (波風 ミナト, Namikaze Minato), known as the Yellow Lightning of Konoha, was a Legendary shinobi who became the Fourth Hokage of Konohagakure. He died sacrificing his life during the attack Nine-Tails in Konoha to seal half the demon in his son, dying heroically in the process.

Will of Fire

Before the age of 24, Minato was chosen to become the Fourth Hokage. in some time during his reign, Minato taught his Flying Thunder God Technique to the Hokage Guard Platoon, to better assist them in their duties of serving the Hokage in certain situations.

Appearance of Minato Namikaze

Minato was a rather tall, fair-skinned man, who on more than one occasion was compared to with Naruto in terms of physical appearance; they both have bright, blue eyes and blonde hair skewered. Minato had his jaws covered in medium-length bangs, framing each side of your face. According to Jiraiya, Minato was considered very beautiful.

Skills of Minato Namikaze

Minato was widely noticed by everyone who watched him or saw him in action, Jiraiya commented. that he was one of the most talented shinobi who ever lived, possessing a talent that could hardly be surpassed by other people.

Personality of Minato Namikaze

Minato was very calm and highly insightful. Even during difficult times he was still able to maintain composure, assessing the situation and coming up with appropriate action plans, a process involving coldness. He also had a playful side, such as naming techniques with extremely complicated names before quickly dismissing them.

Naruto Uzumaki

Who was he?

Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト) is a shinobi from Konohagakure, also being the current reincarnation of Asura and the eponymous protagonist of the Naruto franchise. He became the Kurama's jinchūriki on the day of his birth, a fate that caused him to be condemned to death. ostracized and neglected by the entire village throughout his childhood.

Will of Fire

As Naruto grew into his career as a ninja, his desire to be recognized as a Hokage changed to a desire to help and protect those close to him, which Tobi attributed to the Will of Fire. Naruto developed his nindō in never going back on his path. word.

Naruto Uzumaki's Abilities

Originally, Naruto was a rather inept ninja, having failed the Academy three times. Through sheer determination, however, his abilities rapidly improved to the point of defeating strong shinobi such as Neji Hyūga, Gaara and Kabuto Yakushi, receiving recognition of all Sannin for their potential.

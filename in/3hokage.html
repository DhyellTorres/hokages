Hiruzen Sarutobi

Who was he?

猿飛ヒルゼン, Sarutobi Hiruzen was the Third Hokage (三代目火影, Sandaime Hokage, Literally meaning "Third Fire Shadow") from Konohagakure. Being a disciple of The village's former Hokage, Hiruzen was a powerful ninja, hailed as the God of Shinobi (忍の神, Shinobi no Kami, "The Ultimate Shinobi").

Will of Fire

Knowing that one of them would have to be a decoy and sacrifice himself so the others could escape, Hiruzen volunteers. At the However, Tobirama decides to be the decoy himself, naming Hiruzen as his successor, intensifying the rivalry between his student and Danzō.

Appearance of Hiruzen Sarutobi

Hiruzen was a fair-skinned man of below average height with spiky gray hair. During the period when he trained the Sannin, he had a small goatee. In your youth, he had dark brown hair and tanned skin.

Hiruzen Sarutobi's Skills

Hiruzen was one of the most powerful shinobi in history. In fact, he was so powerful that he became known as the God of Shinobi in his prime. According to Iruka, he claimed to be the strongest Hokage Konoha ever had. He was also one of the Hokage. with the longest life and was the longest in power as Hokage.

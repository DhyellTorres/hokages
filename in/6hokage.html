Kakashi Hatake

Who was he?

Kakashi Hatake (はたけカカシ, Hatake Kakashi) is a shinobi from Konohagakure. He received a Sharingan from his former teammate, Obito Uchiha, when he was younger, making him known as Kakashi the Copy Ninja (コピー忍者のカカシ, Kopī Ninja no Kakashi) and Kakashi do Sharingan.

Will of Fire

In the ninja world, those who break the rules are trash, it's true, but those who abandon their friends are worse than garbage.

Kakashi Hatake's Appearance

Kakashi is a relatively tall shinobi with silver, spiky hair and eyes of a dark. After his first mission as a jōnin, he got a small vertical scar, running over his left eye (which was soon replaced by a Sharingan). Despite yet to be revealed, since childhood, Kakashi has worn a mask over half bottom of the face.

Kakashi Hatake's Abilities

His prodigious talent, skills, and Sharingan prowess have made him one of the most capable ninjas of the village, being recognized throughout the ninja world. He just got named as the leader of Team Kakashi, where he uses his years of experience to train his students. to become skilled ninjas in their own ways.

Friend and rival

Obito Uchiha, Might Guy.

Kakashi Hatake's Personality

Since his childhood, Kakashi was very independent and self-confident, sometimes even appearing being arrogant and condescending; Despite this, Kakashi was very perceptive and intuitive, quickly realizing the situation he was in.
